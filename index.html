Home

Welcome to my website! I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Second Language Studies at the University of Hawai‘i. My research explores how we acquire and process first and additional languages using psycholinguistic methods, with a special focus on (morpho)syntax. I aim to uncover insights that deepen our understanding of language representations and their real-time use.

Outside academia, I enjoy hiking scenic trails and singing my favorite tunes. The background picture was taken on one of the beautiful trails on O‘ahu Island.

Feel free to check out my CV to learn more about me!
